# Versions of OneMuseum

## Pre-Release Versioning

The OneMuseum is under continual development, with new concepts being introduced
in each version and also with bug fixes as one finds in any large software and database
system.

The Pre-release versions are numbers **v 0.n** where n is the pre-release version number.

We may also introduce minor updates, specifically for urgent bug fixes, and these are numbered
**v0.n.m**.

All of the data is preserved between versions, and will also be preserved as we
move into the V1.0 version.

The Pre-Release verions are providing useful tests for us as we take on our first users
and as we gather the experience of these users.

These versions also allow us to implement the facilities in parts, without having to
await for all to be in place before we open up.

## V 0.11.1: 17 December 2022

After another long break in the development work during which we have been extensively 
engaged with museums on their collections, including on the development of ETHER Inventory
and the upgrading of ETHER Base.

No major functional changes at this time. Rather this is ensuring stability
in the release and preparing for the ingest of data to get OneMuseum
set up as the definitive aggregator of museum collections in the country,
throughout Africa, and eventually throughout the world.
