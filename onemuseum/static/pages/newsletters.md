# Newsletters

## Current Newsletter - September 2020

During the past two months we have been running many workshops for museums
during the pandemic lockdown, on a variety of topics.

Fourteen of these workshops were devoted to Museum Education, exploring how this is
changing, and also how to prepare information for the OneMuseum.

There were workshops covering many of the major subjects in the curriculum,
analyzing the specific topics and how these can be established by various museums
to see hw museums can provide educational content.

The most important element of OneMuseum has become the coordination of digital transformation
of museums and schools. This will allow schools access to the exceptional wealth of museum
information to supplement their learning materials.

I would like to appeal to all museums to join us in this venture to make high quality
educational materials available to the schools, so that we can create this bridge betwee
museums, as homes of authentic knowledge, and schools, the homes of learning.

Dr Roger Layton
