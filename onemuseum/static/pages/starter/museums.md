# OneMuseum for Museums

Over the past 15 years since we initially starting working the with museums, we have engaged with more than 100 museums as customers, and have visited many museums during this time.

Museums hold the world's history and much of the world's knowledge, and as an indispensible constituent of civilization. However, most of the information and knowledge has remained hidden to all except those who can visit physically.

Our engagements during this period have been to address how museums must become digital, partly as a survival necessity, on the basis of the broad and extreme position that if you are not online in the future you wil not exist.

Most have been working at their own pace ad preparing their inventories and catalogues, and some engaging in digitization projects, often expensive. Very few museums have gone online with much more than a web-site of social media at this time, and there is little museum products and content available.

We are offering the museum community a
[fee hosting service](../ss/fees)
, on the basis of fair-use, for the hosting of museum content and information products, and a plan for migrating to digital that works at the pace of the museum.

We welcome communication with you and to help you in getting online rapidly.

We can also assist in providing our Spectrum 5 Compliant collection management system, ETHER Base, for your internal collection management needs, as well as a range of other services.
