#  for Non-Museum Collections

Whereas our focus of the OneMuseum has been largely on the needs of museums and their collections there are host of collections that exist within other heritage and scientific institutions which can complement the information within museums, and which can fill gaps in the curriculum for subjects and topics not covered by museums:

* Archives
* Special and Research Libraries
* Galleries
* Scientific Councils
* Research Institutions
* Universities
* Industrial Councils
* Councils for Social Sciences and Education

The approach we take for all institutions is the same as that mentioned in the
[OneMuseum for Museums](../ss/museums)
page.
