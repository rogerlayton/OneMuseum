# OneMuseum Fees

Good news is that there are **no fees** for hosting of content on OneMuseum for all public museums and non-museum collections. Private museums may be charged a cost-related fee.

We are planning to charge for the following services only at present:

* training of the museum personnel on how to get their content ready for upload and ingest into the repository
* conducting the preparation and uploading ourselves on behalf of the museum (the fastest way to get content loaded)
* assisting with museum inventory and cataloguing
* linking your existing collection management system (CMS) to automate the publishing of your content to OneMuseum, which is dependent on access to the databases of the CMS and whether or not it is possible to migrate the information due to differences in formats, schemas, data types and vocabularies.

If you would like to work with us please contact us
[contact us](ss/../contact.md)
.

Our short-term focus is on South African museums, archives, libraries, galleries, and sites, and also on providing educational products, but we are also happy to discuss options in other countries and for producs beyond education (exhibitions, collections, online cataloguing) if these are more important needs.
